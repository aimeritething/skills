Interview me relentlessly about this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Ask questions one at a time, and provide your recommended answer with each.

Peel the onion: start with the big picture — goals, constraints, who it's for — before touching architecture or implementation. Broad questions often reveal that the details need rethinking entirely. If a detail surfaces a gap in the big picture, zoom back out.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
